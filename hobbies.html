Growing up, I've always had a multitude of hobbies, most of which orbit around art and music. I got my first drum kit when I was in Kindergarten and my first guitar shortly after.

In art, I enjoy working with charcoal and watercolors, and I've found a love for more tangible artforms like blacksmithing and wood burning.

Loving art and music as I do, it's really no surprise that I love going to concerts too!
